About Me

I'm Samuel, a data analyst/scientist, and I'm enthusiastic about working to make effective use of data; from the earliest thoughts about what you want to measure and how to reliably gather data, through data preparation, analysis, and management, and finally to meaningful presentation of findings.

I knew I had a love for the tech industry after graduating from the University of Port Hacourt with a degree in petroleum engineering, but deciding on a career was challenging. After some significant self-reflection, my interests were with data, and I've been committed to learning everything about it.
